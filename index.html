About me

Dovah Monah

I am a person with a thousand hobbies, I want to try everything there is to learn! The ability to learn a new skill to create something cool using it is a huge motivator for me.

Pokemon

My love of drawing first started with pokemon, I loved trying to recreate my favourite ones. From there it expanded infinitely.

Original Characters

After discovering D&D with some friends I took to using my characters as subjects for some art experiments with new art styles.

Expanding

Over the years I wanted to learn and experiment with other mediums of creativity too, including knitting, crochet, jewellery and woodwork.

Coding

Eventually my curiosity lead me to coding, which is how this site came to be - my first practice project. I then learned the basics of Python and Kotlin and made some small phone apps.
